# FlowMark (FLOW) — Issuer FAQ

## Do I need HACD to participate?

Yes. Each Stack participation requires 1 HACD per lot according to the FlowMark launch rules. Without HACD you cannot form FLOW or register a StreamMark receipt ID.

## Do I need HAC?

Yes. HAC is required for two things: the stack cost (50 HAC per HACD lot) and the Hacash network transaction fee. Make sure you have enough HAC for both before you begin.

## How much HAC do I need for 1 lot?

50 HAC for the stack cost plus a small network fee. The network fee varies and should be checked at the time of your transaction.

## How many lots can I take?

Between 1 and 10 HACD lots per participant in the public phase.

## How many FLOW will I receive?

10,000 FLOW per HACD lot. If you Stack 3 HACD lots you will receive 30,000 FLOW and 3 StreamMark receipt IDs.

## What is the designated first phase?

The first 32 lots are Stacked by the issuer / designated address to seed genesis StreamMark receipts. The remaining 224 lots are public. All lots follow identical per-lot rules; the only difference is who forms the first 32.

## What is a StreamMark receipt ID?

The unique 6-letter HACD name tied to your lot. It is the non-fungible identity of one stream-anchor receipt. Your fungible FLOW balance and the non-fungible receipt ID are produced by the same single Stack action.

## What happens when all 256 lots are filled?

No more FLOW can ever be formed. Total supply is permanently capped at 2,560,000 FLOW. The Launchpad will show the issuance as completed.

## What happens if I remove my Stack?

Removing the Stack releases your HACD unit. However, the 10,000 FLOW tied to that lot are permanently burned and the StreamMark receipt ID is retired. Your HAC formation cost is not refunded. This is by design — it links FLOW supply and receipt IDs directly to active HACD containers.

## Is FLOW listed on any exchange?

No listing is guaranteed. FlowMark has not arranged any exchange listing. Do not assume FLOW will be tradable on any platform after launch.

## Is this an investment product?

No. FLOW is a HACD Stack Asset for streaming proof. No financial return, price floor, or investment outcome is guaranteed or implied. This is not an investment.

## When will GrowStreams auto-anchoring, revenue-share, and the receipt marketplace be available?

These are planned features that depend on future development. They are not available at launch and are not guaranteed. Follow official FlowMark channels for updates.

## Where can I verify my formation?

All Stack formations are recorded on the Hacash blockchain. You can verify your lot and your StreamMark receipt ID on the Hacash explorer or the HACD Launchpad after your transaction is confirmed.
